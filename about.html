Our Story

About EVO Mobile

"We started EVO Mobile because we believed Sri Lanka deserved a phone shop that treats every customer like an expert."

Founded in 2018 in the heart of Pilimatalawa, EVO Mobile has grown from a small repair bench into one of the island's most trusted smartphone destinations. We carry the latest devices from Apple, Samsung, Google, and more — always unlocked, always genuine. Our certified technicians handle everything from a cracked screen to complex motherboard repairs, with a 90-day service guarantee on every job. We're not a chain. We're your neighbours, and we're here for the long run.

WHAT DRIVES US

Genuine Parts Only

We never cut corners. Every repair uses OEM or certified components.

Fast Turnaround

Most repairs done same day, so you're never long without your device.

90-Day Guarantee

Every repair is backed by a full 90-day warranty, no questions asked.

Honest Advice

We'll tell you when it's cheaper to fix than replace — always.
